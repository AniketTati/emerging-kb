# Internal Vendor Evaluation — Vertex AI Platform

**Author:** Sarah Chen, CFO @ NorthWind Capital
**Date:** April 5, 2026
**Status:** APPROVED — proceed with contract amendment

## Context

NorthWind Capital is evaluating Vertex AI Platform (Vertex Industries Ltd.,
Mumbai) as our primary vendor for document-intelligence services. We
signed the initial Master Services Agreement (MSA) on **Jan 15, 2026**
with an indemnification cap of **$25M** and net-30 payment terms.

After the first quarter of operations, our compliance team
(Rajesh Sharma, our counsel) recommends raising the cap to **$50M** to
reflect the increased document volume now flowing through the Aurangabad
expansion. The amendment was executed on **April 1, 2026**.

## Risks

1. **Indemnification gap during transition.** The amendment is effective
   April 1, 2026 — any incident on or before March 31 is still covered
   under the $25M cap.
2. **Single-vendor concentration.** Vertex now handles 100% of our
   document pipeline. Recommend evaluating a backup vendor in FY27.
3. **Payment-term divergence vs. internal AP policy.** Internal policy
   defaults to net-30. The net-45 in the amendment requires explicit
   exception from the AP committee — minuted on April 3.

## Decision

Approved by the Risk Committee on April 4, 2026. Sarah Chen to
countersign the amendment. Quarterly review with Vertex CEO
Rajesh Sharma scheduled for July 2026.
