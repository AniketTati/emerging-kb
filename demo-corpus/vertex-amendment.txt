AMENDMENT NO. 1 TO MASTER SERVICES AGREEMENT

This Amendment No. 1 (the "Amendment") is entered into as of April 1,
2026 (the "Amendment Effective Date") by and between NorthWind Capital
LLC ("NorthWind"), a Delaware limited liability company, and Vertex
Industries Ltd. ("Vertex"), a Maharashtra company, and amends the
Master Services Agreement between the parties dated January 15, 2026
(the "Original MSA").

RECITALS

WHEREAS, the parties wish to expand the geographic scope of services
to include the Aurangabad facility, in addition to the existing
Mumbai and Pune sites; and

WHEREAS, the parties have agreed to revise the indemnification cap
and payment terms to reflect the increased value and volume of
services;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

1. SCOPE EXPANSION

Schedule A of the Original MSA is hereby amended to include
document-intelligence services at the Vertex Aurangabad facility,
in addition to Mumbai and Pune. The expected monthly document volume
across all three sites is approximately two hundred forty thousand
(240,000) documents.

2. INDEMNIFICATION CAP — AMENDED

Section 4 of the Original MSA is hereby amended in its entirety to
read as follows:

  "Vertex shall indemnify and hold harmless NorthWind from any claims
  arising out of Vertex's gross negligence or willful misconduct,
  provided that Vertex's aggregate liability under this Section 4
  shall not exceed FIFTY MILLION DOLLARS ($50,000,000) per
  occurrence."

This amended cap supersedes the previous cap of twenty-five million
dollars ($25,000,000) set forth in the Original MSA.

3. PAYMENT TERMS — AMENDED

Section 3 of the Original MSA is amended to provide for payment on a
net-forty-five (45) day basis. The previous net-thirty (30) day
payment term is hereby superseded.

4. ALL OTHER TERMS UNCHANGED

Except as expressly amended hereby, all other terms and conditions of
the Original MSA — including without limitation the three-year initial
term, the late-payment interest rate, the confidentiality obligations,
and the Delaware governing law — remain in full force and effect.

5. SIGNATURES

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the parties have executed this Amendment as of
the Amendment Effective Date.
